Contact

To join

Email or call our secretary Sue Wells 01296 429327 for more information or simply turn up.

We rehearse under the conductorship of Graham Wells at Butlers Cross Village Hall near Wendover, Buckinghamshire on Friday evenings between 8pm and 10pm.

To book

Email our secretary Sue Wells with the following information or call 01296 429327 to discuss your ideas

  • Location
  • Start/end time/interval(s)

To perform, we need:

  • Around 15 x 15m of flat ground
  • 20 - 30 chairs (ideally without arms)
  • Parking (and very close drop-off points for drums/tubas)
  • Light refreshments are very welcome
  • Shelter or a wet weather cancellation plan if the venue is outdoors

Please note: We usually take bookings months in advance but sometimes can work with just a few weeks notice. The band is especially busy in the summer and at Christmas time and as an amateur organisation, would not usually be available during the working week.

Payment

We play for the love of making music and do not receive payment as individuals. Our engagement fees pay for our sheet music, rehearsal facilities, instruments and uniform. We aim to cater for most budgets and strive to provide excellent value for money. An appropriate fee will be quoted once we know what you want.
